Text-based mystery game... For some reason, I'm thinking of TES 3: Morrowind as I think about this game. It will be a single player game but very much akin to MUD( Multi-User Dunegons) of old. Quote-Libs!

My first Android application, nearing completion. My partner and I have had the pleasure of working together over the course of two weeks to create this from scratch, and it was quite the learning experience! A random quote is chosen for the user, and they have to input parts of speech, which are then inserted into the quote to make for some funny outcomes.
